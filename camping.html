Camping


The camping opens on 3 August 2024 (Sat) at 10.00AM and closes on 11 August (Sun) at 10.00AM
You can reach the camping from the race area in 2-4 min by car/bike and 5-10 min on foot.

Caravan/Camper/Tent place fee:
85 EUR (fee for whole competition, day rate NOT available)
Personal fee:
40 EUR/person - free for children under 12 years - (fee for the whole competition, day rate is NOT available):
Please note:
the ‘place fee’ needs to be paid by ONLY 1 of the persons staying in the caravan, camper or tent (even if shared with others).
The ‘personal fee’ needs to be paid by ALL persons staying in the camping.
Free camping for children under 12 years also needs to be indicated on the entry form (by writing ‘free’ to the given cell)

It is NOT allowed to camp in the city or around the race area, outside the designated camping zones!
